Hoots Tutoring is a U.Va. student non-profit organization providing an online peer-to-peer tutoring network.


This freelance project included redesigning their website to make it simpler and easy to navigate. The objective was to redesign their previous site to allow for more efficient online booking of tutoring sessions.
Responsive design is key (not only for this mobile-friendly age in general) for college students interacting with the Hoots tutoring site. I aimed to adhere to their brand guidelines to be cohesive, while creating a more pleasing experience for users. Here's the full, responsive prototype.
