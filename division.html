DIVISION 1-ON-1 AIMS TO PROVIDE YOUNG ATHLETES WITH THE OPPORTUNITY TO RECEIVE PERSONAL SPORTS TRAINING FROM NCAA DIVISION I COLLEGE ATHLETES.


The objective of this freelance project was to redesign the previous site to make training sessions simple and easy to book online.

CHALLENGE: redesign the current website (left) to simplify the layout and facilitate easier navigation.

ROLE: designed the website user interface & developed a front end prototype. I began by narrowing down the color scheme and logo of the previous site. The site provides online portal that could allow trainers and trainees to schedule sessions in order to launch their business. an is responsive to mobile interfaces. Alternated title cards guide the users' attention as they navigate down a page.
