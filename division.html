Division 1-on-1 aims to provide young athletes with the opportunity to receive personal sports training from NCAA Division 1 college athletes via online scheduling.


As part of this freelance project, I was tasked with redesigning their website so that it was aesthetically pleasing and easy to navigate across all interfaces, including mobile ones. I started by narrowing down the color scheme of their site and then tailoring the background images to these hues. Then, I simplified the past design to create a clear pattern wireframe while allowing for variation in displaying content. Using my knowledge of composition and perception, I alternated the titles so that the user's vision will be guided across the whole screen while they navigate down the home page.
