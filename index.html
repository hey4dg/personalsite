PROCESS

Fueled by my empathy for others, I approach problems by conducting user research first to examine current and potential frustrations for the problem at hand. Through iterative testing, I aim to improve my designs from sketches on paper to digital solutions throughout all stages of the design process. With simplicity in mind, all interfaces are easy to navigate and responsive to mobile.

ABOUT

HEY, I'm Emily. I graduated from the University of Virginia in May 2018 with a bachelors in Cognitive Science and Studio Art. In addition to painting, illustration, film photography, and UX/UI design, I am passionate about living an authentic and eccentric life filled with asking questions that don't always have answers. My favorite medium is a ballpoint pen, and my favorite color changes daily. Feel free to contact me for price or commissions.

TECHNICAL SKILLS: Proficiency in Java and JavaScript, Adobe Premiere Pro; Fluency in HTML, CSS, Wireframing, Sketch, InVision, Adobe Creative Cloud (InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, Xd).

EXHIBITIONS: "100 Artists for Ella Fitzgerald", Newport News Gallery, VA, 2017
