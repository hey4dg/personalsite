ABOUT

I'm currently in my fourth year at the University of Virginia studying Cognitive Science and Studio Art.
In addition to painting, drawing, darkroom photography, and visual design, I am passionate about asking questions that don't always have answers and aim to foster an empathetic perspective in my daily life.
My favorite medium is a Bic ballpoint pen, and my favorite color changes daily.

TECHNICAL SKILLS: proficiency in Java, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript; fluency in wireframing and Adobe Creative Cloud (InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator).

EXHIBITIONS: "100 Artists for Ella Fitzgerald", Newport News Gallery, Virginia, 2017
