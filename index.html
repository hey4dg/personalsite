PROCESS

My design process is fueled by my empathy for users. I approach problems by trying to understand the user's experience and imagining potential frustrations with interfaces before sketching possible solutions. With simplicity in mind, all designs I create are easy to use and responsive across all interfaces.

ABOUT

I'm currently in my fourth year at the University of Virginia studying Cognitive Science and Studio Art.
In addition to painting, drawing, film photography, and UX/UI design, I am passionate about living an authentic and eccentric life filled with asking questions that don't always have answers. My favorite medium is a Bic ballpoint pen, and my favorite color changes daily.

TECHNICAL SKILLS: Proficiency in Java and JavaScript, Adobe Premiere Pro; Fluency in HTML, CSS, Wireframing, Sketch, InVision, Adobe Creative Cloud (InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, Experience Design).
