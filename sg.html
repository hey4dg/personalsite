SPECIALGUEST IS AN ART AND COMMUNICATION COMPANY BASED IN BROOKLYN THAT AIMS TO BRIDGE THE GAP BETWEEN THE ADVERTISING AND ART REALMS.


In efforts to achieve this goal, I was tasked with designing and developing a prototype for SpecialGuest’s blog (“GuestBook”) with a fellow intern during my internship in summer of 2017.

Our task was to create an icon for the navigation bar that will link to the blog and is visually cohesive with the adjacent social media icons on SpecialGuest's current website. We were also tasked with wireframing and prototyping a blog that adheres with the company's branding style and promotes content around the topics of interest.

The wireframe was co-designed with fellow intern, Isabella Levethan. Due to the timing of the internship, I was unable to see the project to completion; however, my front-end development prototype can be seen here.
